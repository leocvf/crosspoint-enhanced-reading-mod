Build artifact policy note

The PR/BPR tooling rejects binary artifacts in commits.

Do NOT commit firmware binaries (e.g., .bin/.elf/.zip) into this repository.
Instead:
1) Build locally with PlatformIO.
2) Collect artifacts from `.pio/build/default/`.
3) Share artifacts outside git (release assets, cloud link, or CI artifact storage).

Typical output files after a successful build:
- .pio/build/default/firmware.bin
- .pio/build/default/firmware.elf
